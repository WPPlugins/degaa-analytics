== Description ==

** This plugin requires an active account with Degaa Analytics (FREE). Create your free site profile at http://www.degaa.net/signup
to receive your	tracking key and to access your online stats.

Degaa Analytics Wordpress plugin allows for code-less integration into your Wordpress website or 
blog giving real-time, individual visitor tracking and traffic analysis.

Plug-in Features:

* Automatically include Degaa tracking code onto each page
* Ignore Administrators from tracking
* Enable/Disable tracking of Admin pages

Degaa Features:

* Real-time visitor feed
* Accurate Click-stream data
* Traffic Analysis and Summary
* Real-time map overlay and location data
* Individual user profiles and loyalty tracking
* Search engine optimization tracking

== Installation ==

** This plugin requires an active account with Degaa Analytics (FREE). Create your free site profile at http://www.degaa.net/signup
to receive your	tracking key and to access your online stats.

http://www.degaa.net/signup

Manual Installation:

1) Download Degaa.zip to you location compueter

2) Extract the contents of the zip to a local folder

3) Upload the entire contents of the zip to the /plugins/ directory of
   your Wordpress installation

4) Activate the plugin in your plugins manager

5) In the settings menu click on Degaa Analytics

6) Enter the tracking key as provided in your Degaa Analytics welcome email
   note: if you do not have the key available you can find it in the site 
   management settings of the Degaa website by visiting http://www.degaa.net/my-sites

7) Enable the Administrator tracking and Admin tracking features as desired.

e.g.

1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place `<?php do_action('plugin_name_hook'); ?>` in your templates

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

Q. There is no Degaa Analytics section listed on my settings menu

A. Make sure you have activated the plugin in your plugin manager

Q. What is a tracking key??

A. Your tracking key identifies your site on the Degaa Analytics sytem and is required to
   start tracking your visitors.  To obtain your key sign up free at http://www.degaa.net/signup

Q. I can't find my Tracking Key

A. Your tracking key can be found on the website management page page
   on Degaa Analytics.  Visit http://www.degaa.net/my-sites and click on 'Settings'
   for your site.  If you continue to have problems email support@degaa.net
